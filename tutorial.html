Welcome to PANDORÆ
 
arrow_downward
This brief tutorial will show you what PANDORÆ does.

It will allow you to see the extent of what it doesn't do (yet). 
 
arrow_downward
PANDORÆ is a data exploration tool.

It has been designed to investigate how science sometimes changes humans. 
 
It can even be used to show that science often doesn't.
 
Some people use PANDORÆ to build their literature reviews.
 
arrow_downward
PANDORÆ allows you to perform three main operations:

Load data
Enrich data
Explore data

 
arrow_downward
Chapter 1: Loading data

PANDORÆ enables you to load data from different types of sources.

Those sources can be online databases that aggregate documents such as scientific articles.

You can also add any kind of document published by States, institutions, newspapers or even a blog post. Those can be related or unrelated to science articles.

PANDORÆ also supports some specific online public debate platforms and social-media data.
